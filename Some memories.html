Some Memories:

One of my earliest childhood memory was when I received my first award when I was in L.K.G which was The General Proficiency Award, which was given to the student who scored the highest marks among all the sections of the same standard.Another memory was when I was made a Captain fo my group in my school where all my teachers and my friends unanimously supported me and elected me.

The proudest memory for me and my parents was when I scored 10/10 GPA in my SSC Board and when I was called upon the stage and presented a memento for my success and hardwork. A few other memories include when I participated in group dance and we started the practice just the evening before the competition day and we composed steps ourselves and practised only that evening and the next day Oh My God!! we bagged the second position.That is an awesome memory.

A few sweet and cool memories are of my college with my besties Srinija, Sharvani, Supraja.Srinija and I share many lovely moments and we used to do all things together so much that we were called DD Colony Sister's by my sir.I really miss all those days now.While coming to Roorkee my vulnerable memory was travelling from Delhi to Roorkee where we walked almost 2kms in the hot red sun carrying our own luggage following the person who told that he would take us to the bus. After coming to Roorkee my first memory is when I bought my cycle.
